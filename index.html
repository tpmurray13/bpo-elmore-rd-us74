Analyst notes & caveats

  • On-market comps carry primary weight per client instruction. Anchor comps: Dixie Guano, Laurinburg (25 ac / $31,200/ac / I-1 / Scotland Co.) and US-74 Alt, Lumberton (58.47 ac / $17,000/ac / HI / US-74 frontage). These bracket the adjusted midpoint at $25,000/ac.
  • Closed sales (RA rural land) confirm floor only. All three CoStar closed sales ($2,216–$7,000/ac) are RA-zoned rural or agricultural transactions — not commercial comps. They are not used to set the value ceiling.
  • ZV Pate Inc. (Joes Creek Rd seller) appears as adjacent parcel owner on subject's recorded plat. Worth noting in any due diligence or disposition context.
  • Utility gap vs. anchor comp. Dixie Guano (OM-1, $31,200/ac) is a previously developed lot with electricity on-site. Subject has no confirmed water, sewer, gas, or streets — the primary driver of the downward adjustment to $25,000/ac.
  • Wetland risk. Aerial shows a possible pond/wet area in the SW corner. Wetland delineation is strongly recommended before listing. Any jurisdictional wetlands reduce net usable acreage.
  • Tract 1 (7.00 ac) excluded. A combined 31.81 ac offering could affect per-acre pricing and buyer pool.
  • Corporate seller process. CMH Homes Inc. (Berkshire Hathaway) may require internal approvals to list or contract. Confirm decision-making authority early.
